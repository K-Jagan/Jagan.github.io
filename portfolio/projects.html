Projects

  • E-Placement Preparation Platform

    In today's competitive job market, possessing both strong technical skills and effective communication abilities is paramount for job seekers. This advanced iteration of the e-placement preparation platform is a mobile application designed to provide comprehensive technical training and foster the development of communication skills.

    • Inspired by the success of platforms such as Duolingo and Geek for Geeks, our platform integrates gamification elements.
    • Includes advanced features such as voice assistance, MCQ tests, coding tests, and error resolution.
    • Aims to offer users a holistic learning experience, equipping them with necessary skills and knowledge for successful placement interviews.
    • Technologies used: Flutter, Figma, Express.js, MongoDB, and external API integration.
